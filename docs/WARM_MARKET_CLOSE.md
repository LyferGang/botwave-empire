## THE CLOSE SCRIPT (Family/Friend Version)

### Step 1: The Ask (No Pitch)

```
YOU: "Hey [name], I need your help with something."

THEM: "Sure, what is it?"

YOU: "I built a phone answering system for [their trade]. It answers calls 24/7, books jobs, dispatches you. No AI jargon - it just answers your phone."

THEM: "Oh, like an answering service?"

YOU: "Yeah, but cheaper. And it actually books the job, not just takes a message."
```

### Step 2: The Demo (Show The Loss)

```
YOU: "Call your business phone right now."

(THEY CALL - voicemail or they don't answer)

YOU: "That call just went to voicemail. If it was a real customer needing [their service], they'd call your competitor. That's $[their job value] lost."

YOU: "Now call my Botwave number."

(BOT ANSWERS INSTANTLY)

BOT: "Thanks for calling [their trade]. What do you need?"

YOU: "See? It answered. It asked questions. It's ready to book the job for you."
```

### Step 3: The Math (Their Numbers)

```
YOU: "How many calls do you miss per week?"

THEM: "Maybe 5-10?"

YOU: "Each call is $[their job value]. That's $[weekly loss]/week lost."

YOU: "My system is $[their price]/month. One captured call pays for it. Rest is profit."

YOU: "I'm looking for 10 [their trade] to test it. You'd be first. I'll set it up free. You just try it 30 days."

THEM: "What if it doesn't work?"

YOU: "I'll fix it in 5 minutes. I'm on call 24/7 for you."
```

### Step 4: The Close (Assume Yes)

```
YOU: "What's your business phone number? I'll forward it to Botwave tonight."

THEM: "It's 555-0123."

YOU: "Perfect. I'll have it running by tomorrow morning. You'll get a text when a job comes in."

YOU: "After 30 days, if you want to keep it, it's $[price]/month. If not, I'll turn it off. No hard feelings."

THEM: "Sounds good."
```
