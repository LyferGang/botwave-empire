### Objection Handling

**"We already have an answering service."**
→ "Great! What are you paying? Most services charge $800-1500/month. We do the same at $399 - and we never sleep, never call in sick."

**"We handle calls ourselves."**
→ "Totally get it. But how many calls go to voicemail after hours? That's $300-500 per call going to competitors. We capture all of them at $199/month beta pricing."

**"We're too busy to try something new."**
→ "Exactly why you need this. You're losing revenue from calls you can't answer. This captures that revenue without adding headcount."

**"Send me info."**
→ "Happy to. But let me ask - what's the #1 thing you'd want to see? Is it pricing, how it works, or case studies?"

**"We'll think about it."**
→ "Of course. Quick question - what's the main concern? Is it pricing, setup time, or something else?"
